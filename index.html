About Me

As a student of both dance and data, I believe in the power of technology to expand the horizon of performance arts. I focus on creating intuitive dashboards that help audiences and creators understand the ecosystem of dance performances. My practice integrates movement theory with quantitative analysis to provide new insights into the performing arts industry.
